Tsavo Holidays

Overview

Tsavo Holidays Overview The park is divided into East and West sections. Two rivers, which are the Athi and Tsavo River converge at the park which then becomes the Galana River. The park is mainly a grassland which easily thrives due to the Hot and dry climate of the Region, temperatures fluctuates between 310 c during the day and 200 c during the night.

Natural Attractions

Tsavo National Park is home to different species of Wild animals that mainly consists of elephants, leopard, Black rhino, olive baboons, Spotted hyenas, colobus monkeys, serval cat, blue duiker and many others. This rich wildlife forms an epic scenary for tourist attraction in the region, hence it's among the best national parks in Kenya.
