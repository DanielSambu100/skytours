Amboseli Holidays

Overview

The Amboseli is the second most fascinating destination after the Masai Mara and its well known for its numerous herds of elephants also known as the “floating Jumbos” from how the elephants look while walking through the swamps located inside the park. Amboseli National Park is home to a variety of wildlife and birds such as the elephants which are abundant in the park, giraffe, hippo, buffalo, zebra, wildebeest, hyena, Grants and Thompson Gazelles. With over 420 different bird species present at the park.

Natural Attractions

Being the home to a variety of wildlife species, Amboseli National Park is one of the best destination for game viewing. A part from the great spectacular view of mount Kilimanjaro, the permanent swamps inside the park are a common destination for tourist to view the elephant wallow in the mad or just quench their thirst. The observation hill offers a magical view of the savannah and the park in general, enabling a greater view of the sunsets and sunrise apart from seeing the variety of wildlife Amboseli has to offer.
