Shanzu Beach Holidays

Overview

Basking in over 320 days of sunshine a year, Shanzu beach is a Sunshine Coast. Shanzu holidays are as you want them to be. Family-friendly resorts, offer everything you could possibly need if you’re taking a break with the kids, while couples might prefer the tranquillity of resorts. with a visit to the cobbled streets of Mombasa’s Old Town. while those who’d like to get some sightseeing done will find plenty to see and do.

Place to stay

Shanzu is home to some of the world’s best beach resorts, offer some of the finest experience and are well worth visiting.

Natural Attraction

Shanzu Beach provides an excellent base for exploring the sights of Mombasa in-depth. With a wide number of attractions to discover, this sought-after part of town will not cease to amaze you. An undeniably popular travel destination, Shanzu Beach offers a memorable escape with its famous sites and fine dining.
