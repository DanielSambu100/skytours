Nyali Beach Holidays

Overview

Warm sunshine, stunning sandy coves, and friendly tavernas await on your Nyali holiday, making this place a classic beach holiday destination. Whether you want to chill out and relax in a luxurious resort to enjoy some me-time, or are looking forward to splashing in a warm sea with the children, you'll find something for everyone on this sunshine beach in the Nyali.

Places to Stay

Lively resorts covers the north-coast corner of Mombasa with fine sandy beaches and plenty of family-friendly facilities, where you can tuck into some fresh seafood in waterside restaurants.

Natural Attractions

Nyali beach is at the top of the list for most holidaymakers. If you don't want to spend your entire holiday on a beach towel, Nyali could still be the destination for you. The beach is also known for its nearby Parks.
